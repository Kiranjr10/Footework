Dasarath Stadium

Capacity: 25,000

Named after King Dasarath of Nepal, the stadium has witnessed countless historic events, from national football championships to international tournaments like the South Asian Games. The stadium stands as a testament to Nepal's passion for sports and its continuous efforts to develop infrastructure that promotes athletic excellence.
