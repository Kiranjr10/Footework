FC Barcelona – Based in Spain, Barcelona is one of the most successful clubs in world football, known for its tiki-taka playing style and legendary players like Messi, Xavi, and Iniesta. The club has won multiple La Liga and Champions League titles, playing at the iconic Spotify Camp Nou.

Real Madrid – The most successful club in Champions League history, Real Madrid is known for its dominance in Spain and Europe. With legendary players like Cristiano Ronaldo, Zidane, and Raul, the club has a rich history and plays at the famous Santiago Bernabéu Stadium.

Bayern Munich – The powerhouse of German football, Bayern Munich has dominated the Bundesliga for decades. With multiple Champions League titles and world-class players like Müller, Lewandowski, and Lahm, they play at the modern Allianz Arena.

Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) – France’s top club, PSG has risen to global fame with stars like Neymar, Mbappé, and Messi. The club dominates Ligue 1 and plays at the Parc des Princes in Paris.

Liverpool FC – One of England’s most successful clubs, Liverpool has a rich history in European and domestic football. Known for its passionate fans at Anfield and players like Gerrard, Salah, and Dalglish, the club has won multiple Premier League and Champions League titles.

Manchester City – A rising force in English football, Man City has dominated the Premier League in recent years under Pep Guardiola. With world-class players and a strong tactical approach, they play at the Etihad Stadium.

Manchester United – One of the most globally supported clubs, Man United has a legendary history with players like Beckham, Ronaldo, and Rooney. With numerous league and European titles, the club plays at Old Trafford, known as the "Theatre of Dreams."

Borussia Dortmund – Known for its electrifying atmosphere at Signal Iduna Park, Dortmund is a top German club famous for its attacking football and youth development. The club has won multiple Bundesliga titles and produced stars like Haaland and Reus.

Inter Milan – A dominant force in Italian football, Inter has won Serie A and Champions League titles. Known for legendary players like Zanetti, Ronaldo (Brazil), and Lukaku, they share the San Siro Stadium with AC Milan.

AC Milan – One of Italy’s most decorated clubs, AC Milan has a rich history in European and domestic football. With legends like Maldini, Kaka, and Shevchenko, they have won multiple Champions League and Serie A titles, playing at San Siro.

Dasarath Stadium – Nepal’s largest and most iconic stadium, Dasarath Stadium is located in Kathmandu and hosts domestic and international matches. It serves as the home ground for the Nepal national football team and various club tournaments.
